## The Problem with Stock Android
Google Play Services is deeply integrated and sends significant telemetry. Manufacturer skins add further tracking.

## Privacy-Focused Android Distributions
- **[GrapheneOS](https://grapheneos.org)** - hardened Android for Pixel devices; gold standard; sandboxed Google Play available
- **[CalyxOS](https://calyxos.org)** - includes microG; more user-friendly
- **[DivestOS](https://divestos.org)** - supports more devices; privacy-patched LineageOS fork

## Minimum Hardening on Stock Android
- Aggressively deny permissions
- Use a DNS-based ad blocker (NextDNS, AdGuard)
- Use a VPN
- Disable or uninstall unused Google apps
