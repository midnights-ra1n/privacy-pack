# Cloud Storage
> 🟢 Beginner

## The Problem with Google Drive / Dropbox
- Provider can read your files
- Subject to government requests
- Business model may involve data analysis

## Privacy-Respecting Alternatives
| Service | Zero-Knowledge | Open Source | Free Tier |
|---------|---------------|-------------|-----------|
| [Proton Drive](https://proton.me/drive) | ✅ | Partial | 1 GB |
| [Filen](https://filen.io) | ✅ | ✅ | 10 GB |
| [Tresorit](https://tresorit.com) | ✅ | ❌ | No |

## Encrypt Before Uploading
Use **Cryptomator** to encrypt files locally before they sync. The cloud provider only ever sees encrypted blobs.

## Self-Hosted Alternative
**Nextcloud** full Google Drive replacement. See [07-self-hosting/getting-started.md](../07-self-hosting/getting-started.md).
