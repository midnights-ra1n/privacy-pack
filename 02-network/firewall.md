# Firewalls
> 🟡 Intermediate / 🔴 Advanced for router-level

## Why It Matters
Outbound filtering lets you see and block apps sending telemetry or unexpected network requests.

## Per-Device Tools
- **Linux:** UFW (inbound), Portmaster (per-app outbound)
- **macOS:** Little Snitch (paid) or Lulu (free, open-source)
- **Windows:** Portmaster

## Router/Network Level
- **OPNsense** - open-source, FreeBSD-based, feature-rich
- **pfSense** - long-standing alternative
- **GL.iNet** - consumer routers with OpenWrt, good for travel
