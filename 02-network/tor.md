# Tor
> 🟡 Intermediate — Powerful anonymity tool requiring discipline.

## How It Works
Traffic routes through 3 relays. Each only knows previous and next hop.

## Tor Browser
Download from [torproject.org](https://www.torproject.org). Use **Safest** security level for sensitive activities.

## What Tor Protects Against
✅ ISP seeing what you visit  
✅ Websites seeing your real IP  

## What It Doesn't Protect Against
❌ Logging into personal accounts (immediately de-anonymizes you)  
❌ Malicious exit nodes on unencrypted traffic (use HTTPS always)  

## Tor vs VPN
| Use Case | Tor | VPN |
|----------|-----|-----|
| Anonymity | ✅ Strong | ❌ Weak |
| Speed | ❌ Slow | ✅ Fast |
| Daily browsing | ❌ | ✅ |
| High-risk journalism | ✅ | ❌ |
