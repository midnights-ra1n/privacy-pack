# VPNs
> 🟢 Beginner setup / 🟡 Advanced configuration

## What a VPN Does (and Doesn't Do)
✅ Hides traffic from your ISP  
✅ Replaces your IP from the website's perspective  
✅ Protects you on untrusted networks  

❌ Does not make you anonymous  
❌ Does not protect against browser fingerprinting  
❌ Does not hide identity if you're logged into accounts  

## How to Choose
- **Audited no-log policy** - claims mean nothing without a published audit
- **Open-source client**
- **WireGuard support**
- **Kill switch**
- **Accepts anonymous payment** (cash, Monero)

🚩 Red flags: free VPNs, no audit, owned by ad-tech companies
