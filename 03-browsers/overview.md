# Browser Overview
> 🟢 Beginner

## Why Avoid Chrome
Google's business is advertising. Chrome sends telemetry and has resisted features that interfere with ad tracking.

## Comparison
| Browser | Base | Default Privacy | Fingerprint Resistance | uBlock Origin |
|---------|------|-----------------|----------------------|---------------|
| Chrome | Chromium | ❌ Poor | ❌ Poor | ⚠️ Degraded |
| Firefox | Gecko | 🟡 Moderate | 🟡 Moderate | ✅ Full |
| Brave | Chromium | ✅ Good | ✅ Good | ⚠️ Degraded |
| LibreWolf | Gecko | ✅ Very good | ✅ Good | ✅ Full |
| Mullvad Browser | Gecko | ✅ Excellent | ✅ Excellent | ⚠️ Avoid |
| Safari | WebKit | ✅ Good | 🟡 Moderate | ❌ No (Use AdGuard extension or NextDNS instead)|

## Recommendations
- **No effort:** Brave
- **Power user:** Firefox + hardening
- **Pre-hardened:** LibreWolf
- **Max fingerprint protection:** Mullvad Browser
- **macOS ecosystem:** Safari

## Browser Isolation Strategy
Use different browsers for different contexts: one for logged-in accounts, one for general browsing, Tor for sensitive use.
