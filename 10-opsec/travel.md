# Travel & Border Crossing
> 🟡 Intermediate

## Border Risks
Border agents in many countries (US, UK, Australia) have broad powers to demand device access without a warrant.

## Before Traveling
- Consider a **clean travel device** with minimal data
- **Disable biometric unlock** before border control you can often be compelled to provide a fingerprint, not a passcode
- Remove sensitive apps and history

## On Untrusted Networks
- Always use a VPN on hotel/airport Wi-Fi
- Prefer mobile data over unknown Wi-Fi

## High-Risk Travel
- Leave usual devices at home
- Use Tails OS on a USB drive
- Use Signal with disappearing messages
- Assume hotel rooms may be monitored

## Device Practices
- Full disk encryption before travel
- Strong PIN, not biometrics at the border
