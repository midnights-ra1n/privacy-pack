# Social Media & OSINT
> 🟢 Beginner

## The OSINT Risk
Your public social media presence can reveal: home neighborhood, workplace, daily routine, relationships, political views.

## Practical Reductions
- Audit privacy settings on every platform
- Remove location data from photos before posting
- Don't tag your location in real time
- Separate personal and professional accounts
- Use pseudonyms for accounts not requiring your real identity

## Remove Photo Metadata
Images contain EXIF data (GPS, device, timestamp):
- `exiftool -all= photo.jpg`
- Scrambled Exif (Android)
- Metapho (iOS)

## Decentralized Alternatives
- **Mastodon** Twitter/X alternative
- **Pixelfed** Instagram alternative
- **Lemmy** Reddit alternative
