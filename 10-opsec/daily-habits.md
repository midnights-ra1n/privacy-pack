# Daily Habits
> 🟢 Beginner - Small habits, high impact.

## Account Hygiene
- Unique email alias per service (SimpleLogin or AnonAddy)
- Password manager : never reuse passwords
- 2FA everywhere, preferably TOTP or hardware key
- Delete accounts you no longer use

## Device Habits
- Keep OS and apps updated
- Auto-lock screen (short timeout)
- Full disk encryption enabled
- Don't use unknown USB chargers (juice jacking risk)

## Network Habits
- VPN on public/untrusted Wi-Fi
- Encrypted DNS
- Prefer HTTPS & check the URL before entering credentials

## Browsing
- uBlock Origin installed
- Don't click links in emails navigate directly
- Separate browser profiles per context

## Communication
- Signal for sensitive conversations
- Remember: the other person's OPSEC is outside your control
