# Anonymous Payments
> 🟡 Intermediate - Check laws in your jurisdiction.

## Cash
Still works. Use it.

## Prepaid Cards
Available in many countries. KYC requirements vary by jurisdiction and card value.

## Privacy.com (US Only)
Virtual debit cards per merchant. Not anonymous from Privacy.com itself, but protects your real card from breaches.
[privacy.com](https://privacy.com)

## Cryptocurrency
**Bitcoin is NOT anonymous.** The blockchain is fully public and traceable.

**Monero (XMR)** is the privacy-focused option:
- Ring signatures hide sender
- Stealth addresses hide recipient
- RingCT hides amounts

Note: regulations around privacy coins are tightening in some jurisdictions.
