# Passkeys
> 🟢 Beginner - Emerging standard replacing passwords.

## What Are Passkeys?
Your device stores a private key; the website stores the public key. Login is done by signing a challenge. Your private key never leaves your device.

## Benefits
- No password to steal or phish
- Immune to credential stuffing
- No shared secret on the server

## Current State
- Supported by: Apple, Google, Microsoft, Bitwarden, 1Password
- Works on: Google, GitHub, Apple, Microsoft, PayPal, and more
- Growing but not universal yet

## Limitations
- Portability between platforms is awkward
- Recovery depends on the service if you lose your device

## Recommendation
Use passkeys where available with a cross-platform manager like Bitwarden. Keep password + 2FA as fallback.
