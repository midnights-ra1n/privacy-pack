# Anonymity Levels
> 🟢 Understanding the spectrum from privacy to anonymity.

**Privacy** - Content is hidden, identity is not.
**Pseudonymity** - Consistent alias, not linked to real name.
**Anonymity** - Actions cannot be linked to identity at all.

## Comparison
| Property | Privacy | Pseudonymity | Anonymity |
|----------|---------|--------------|-----------|
| Hides content | ✅ | Depends | ✅ |
| Hides identity | ❌ | Partially | ✅ |
| Long-term sustainability | ✅ | With discipline | Very hard |
| Tools needed | E2EE | Alias + VPN | Tor + strict OPSEC |

## Common Mistakes
- Assuming a VPN gives anonymity
- Logging into personal accounts over Tor
- Confusing encryption with anonymity
- Underestimating behavioral fingerprinting
