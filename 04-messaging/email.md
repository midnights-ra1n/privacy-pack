# Email Privacy
> 🟢 Beginner

## The Fundamental Limitation
Email wasn't designed for privacy. Metadata (sender, recipient, subject, timestamps) is almost always exposed.

## Privacy-Focused Providers
| Provider | E2EE | Open Source | Jurisdiction |
|----------|------|-------------|--------------|
| [Proton Mail](https://proton.me) | ✅ | Partial | Switzerland |
| [Tuta](https://tuta.com) | ✅ | ✅ | Germany |

## Email Aliases
Use a different alias per service to prevent tracking and protect your real address.
- [SimpleLogin](https://simplelogin.io) - open-source, Proton-owned
- [AnonAddy](https://addy.io) - open-source, self-hostable

## Why Avoid Gmail/Outlook
- Content historically scanned for advertising
- Subject to government requests
- No zero-knowledge encryption - provider can read your mail
