# Instant Messaging
> 🟢 Beginner

## What to Look For
- E2EE enabled by default
- Open-source and audited
- Minimal metadata collection
- Ideally no phone number required

## Comparison
| App | E2EE Default | Open Source | Phone Required | Metadata |
|-----|-------------|-------------|----------------|----------|
| Signal | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | Minimal |
| Session | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | Very minimal |
| SimpleX | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | Near zero |
| Matrix/Element | ✅ (opt-in) | ✅ | ❌ | Moderate |
| WhatsApp | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ | High (Meta) |
| Telegram | ❌ by default | Partial | ✅ | High |

## Recommendations
- **General use:** Signal
- **No phone number:** SimpleX or Session
- **Decentralized/self-hosted:** Matrix
- **Avoid for sensitive use:** Telegram (no E2EE in group chats by default)
